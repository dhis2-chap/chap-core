#Effectively learn modelling using Chap

CHAP is designed to allow model developers to focus on their core modelling idea, and then rely on Chap for rich support of data, evaluation, interoperability and more.

We here provide guides for implementing custom models in *Python* and *R*. The recommended flow is slightly different for the two different languages, but the general idea is the same.

We have a separate guide for those who [already know modelling](experienced_modeller.md), while we here provide guides based on example code to 

* [Learn both theoretical and practical aspects of modelling, leaning on Chap](learn_modelling.md)
